1. Executive Summary

I served as the Student Lead of IEDC at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, Kottayam (KSUMIEDC1076) from 1 July 2025 to 31 January 2026. During this seven-month period, my primary focus was to strengthen the startup culture on campus by encouraging innovation, mentoring ideas, and creating consistent engagement opportunities for students. I coordinated and conducted 36 events covering entrepreneurship awareness, technical skill development, innovation workshops, and startup mentoring sessions. These initiatives engaged more than 700 students, with an average participation of around 40 students per event. A key part of my responsibility was mentoring student ideas.Through programs like Ideathon and PitchFest 1.0, I mentored around 20 ideas, of which 6 showed strong startup potential. Currently, 5 ideas are under active mentorship, and 2 are progressing towards MVP development. I also supported and successfully registered one student startup, RedLine, and facilitated workspace support through the TBI. This achievement encouraged more students to actively pursue entrepreneurship.To build a sustainable ecosystem, I identified and brought together 56 interested students to work alongside IEDC as a parallel team, ensuring continued momentum even beyond my tenure. I also guided students to participate in KSUM initiatives such as PitchFest and cluster-level hackathons. Although building a startup culture was initially challenging, steady mentoring, visible outcomes, and consistent activities gradually increased student confidence and participation. My efforts were aimed not just at organizing events, but at creating a lasting innovation ecosystem within the institution.
